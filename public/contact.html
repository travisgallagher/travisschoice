Who is Travis?


I am a current student at Devmountain, who created Travis's Choice as his first project!
I also work as a Critical Care Technician in the Cardiac ICU at Intermountain Medical Center.
Me and my wife can never decide where to eat at.
I came up with the idea, after all of the nurses I work with also couldn't decide on what to eat for lunch.

My solution: create a website that chooses where you eat for you!
Thank you Cardiac ICU nurses for helping me with this idea, you're the best!
