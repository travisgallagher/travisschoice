What is Travis's choice?

Travis's choice is random restaurant selector.
Pick which restaurants sound good, and I will decide which one you eat at!

Instructions:

  1. Enter your zipcode
  2. Select the number of restaurants you would like to look at
  3. Push the "load restaurants" button
  4. Select the restaurants you want added to your list of possible meal choices
  5. Push the "Travis Pick" button, and I will select which restaurant you eat at!
